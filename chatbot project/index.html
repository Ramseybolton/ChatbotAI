Quick Safety Tips

Essential electrical safety guidelines

Use Surge Protectors

Protect your electronics from voltage spikes by using quality surge protectors for valuable devices.

Avoid Overloading

Don't plug too many devices into one outlet. Distribute the electrical load across multiple circuits.

Check Cords Regularly

Inspect power cords for fraying, damage, or exposed wires. Replace damaged cords immediately.

Proper Wattage

Always use bulbs with the correct wattage for your fixtures to prevent overheating.

Keep Water Away

Never use electrical appliances near water or with wet hands to avoid electric shock.

Install GFCIs

Use Ground Fault Circuit Interrupters in bathrooms, kitchens, and outdoor outlets for safety.

Electrical Emergency?

In case of immediate danger, turn off the main power supply and call emergency services.
